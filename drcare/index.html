About Rent (Remote Nursing Telecommunication)

Where Care meets Patient

Migrating to any new town or country can be cumbersome and quite difficult for any person to adapt to the habitat and its weather, food, etc. Searching for a new doctor can be stressful-especially if you are managing a chronic condition or if a decision has to be made quickly. ReNT provides a solution to these challenges by remotely connecting to your family doctor and the doctor(s) in turn connects with the local or international hospital nurses to address the patient in need.

Doctor Teleconsultation

Consult the doctor you trust the most using our video calling or chat service.

Doctor to Local Hospital Communication

Your Doctor will contact your local hospital and provide them with patient's PHR and eRx.

Nurse Selection

Hospital inturn will select the nurse available that is best suited to provide required care to the patient.

Care Delivery

Nurse will reach patient's address and perform the necessary task to ensure patient's well being.
